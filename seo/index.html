SEO

Customised SEO Packages

Our SEO packages include a fully comprehensive search engine optimisation strategy, including competitive keyword research, content writing and SEO engineering to ensure your site ranks as high as it can on major search engines – and stays there.

A high ranking website includes SEO optimised content, striking design and innovative programming. Our team of designers, programmers and SEO strategists have built hundreds of websites that combine those elements.

We understand the complex algorithms and criteria used by major engines to rank websites and stay up to date with their ever changing rules. Let us put our expertise to work for you. While no one can – or should – guarantee you top ranking on a search engine page, we can help you make your site as effective as it can be.

Our goal is to get you the most visibility of all major search engines using your keyword criteria. It’s not magic, it’s knowing how a search engine thinks- we know what works!

Benefit from a customised SEO package that includes its all:

Keyword and Competitor Analysis

  • Industry specific competitive keyword analysis
  • Analysis of your competitors’ websites, marketing strategies and keywords used

Onsite Optimisation

  • Generating optimised content and/or guiding and tweaking provided content
  • Optimising targeted keywords in high visibility zones: H tags (heading tags), image alt tags and in-document jump links
  • Adjusting HTML code of each page to assist search engine ranking
  • Inbound links from trustworthy websites that are related to your company but are not direct competition.

Off-site Optimisation

  • Outbound links on high traffic websites to improve the credibility of your website and increase online brand reputation and search engine ranking

Sitemap.xml file

  • Optimisation of sitemap file and indexing and the priority of each page

Robots.txt File

  • Selective Implementation of Robots.txt files. This tells search engines which files to index and which should not be indexed and ranked

Website Analytics & Tools

  • Analysis of the performance of your website using Google Analytics and Web Master to track where visitors are coming from, going to, and the reasons for their behaviour on your website

Monthly SEO Feedback

  • Monthly feedback on your website performance, visibility, and keywords targeted
