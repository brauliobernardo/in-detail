The Talent

Gerald Yapp

The Boss / Creative Director

Started a career in design before becoming a pop-idol was offered as a viable work alternative. He once designed a brand new colour. Won some awards – some even for design. Worked on Apple before it was even cool. Constantly striving to eradicate Comic Sans in all its point sizes. Like all 80s kids, he was always being told how special he was and didn’t get that that it went for everybody else too. Now he’s depressed that he’s not more important. Currently treating a nasty running addiction with cold beer.

Sarita Clegg

The Other Boss / Designer

Sarita is a multidisciplinary designer with an eye for detail and pixel perfection. Sarita holds a Bachelor’s degree in Information Science from the University of Stellenbosch as well as a Bachelor’s Degree in Graphic Design from the Open Window School of visual communication. She has spent the last 10 years working professionally in Johannesburg. Although her skill set is extensive, her greatest expertise revolves around the evolution of brand identity design and print collateral. Her wish is to combine her knowledge and experience in these areas to deliver the best creative to her clients and their audiences. She also loves coffee.
